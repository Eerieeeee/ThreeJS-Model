Sam Truesdale


I create environments, convey narratives,
delve deep into horror and cook it up in the kitchen daily.

I have recently graduated from the School of Simulation and Visualisation within the Glasgow School of Art with a B.Sc. (Hons) in Immersive System Design - 3D Modelling (2:1), I have spent the over half of a decade building up multidisciplinary skills in order to pursue my driving passion of creating horror inspired 3D environments.

Environments and worlds are so much more than just set pieces for players to walk through to reach their next goal. They are active participants in the overarching narrative, a separate character in the story. Painting a picture in the players mind and also filling them with emotions. I pour my heart into creating environments that will ground the players in a world and allow them to connect emotionally with the world around them.
